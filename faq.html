Frequently asked questions

FAQ

The most common questions

Our team is well trained and highly qualified experts in professional cleaning and disinfect services to keep all types of premises clean and safe.

How can I get a quote?


Either fill out one of the forms in our website, or you can click the schedule now button found on our home page. It will allow you to schedule time with us to have your estimate done.

Are cleaning supplies included?


Cleaning supplies aren’t included by default. This ensures your cleaner uses the products you like and there isn’t any cross-contamination from house to house.

What if I'm not happy with my cleaning?


Please get in touch with us right away and we’ll make arrangements to rectify the situation. We have a satisfaction guarantee that we stand by.

What services do you offer?


We offer a range of services from weekly cleaning to one-time cleaning to move-in and move-out cleaning. We cover residential, short-term rentals, and commercial. We also do home organization and hoarding cleanup. You can see the different services by visiting the front page and clicking on the “Our Services” button in the drop-down navigation menu.

Is it necessary to get an estimate?


Yes. We have to do estimates to be able to give you an accurate price. Each home varies in different ways, so it is impossible for us to quote a price, sight unseen.

How many cleaners do you send?


That honestly depends on the size of the job. For small jobs, we will send one cleaner. For medium jobs, we will send two. For large jobs, we will try to send three cleaners when possible.

What is included in home organization?


It all depends on the client's needs. Typically, we'll sort through your disorganized items and neatly place them in the designated area with clear labels for easy access. Our aim is to create a clutter-free environment.

Do I need to pre-clean before you come?


No pre-cleaning necessary. That’s what we are for. The only thing we ask is that any and all illegal documents or substances be out of sight. We don’t care what you do but we DO NOT want to know about it! And, for the safety of myself and my cleaners, make sure there are no needles lying around or in the trash. Please place those objects in a coffee can or coke bottles so we don’t get poked.
